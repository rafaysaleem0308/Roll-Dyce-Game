Game Instructions

Welcome to the Roll-Dyce Game! Here are the instructions to play:

  1. Starting the Game:
    • Click on the New game button to reset the game and start afresh.

  2. Rolling the Dice:
    • Click the Roll dice button to roll the dice.
    • The result of the dice roll will be displayed.
    • If you roll a number other than 1, it will be added to your current score.
    • If you roll a 1, your current score will reset to 0 and it will be the next player's turn. But your Previous Score will not get zero

  3. Holding Your Score:
    • Click the Hold button to add your current score to your total score.
    • After holding, it will be the next player's turn.
    • If your total score reaches 100 or more, you win the game.
    • Using the hold button is strategic. If you roll a 1, your current score will reset to 0, so hold your score to save it and avoid this risk.

  4. Winning the Game:
    • The first player to reach a total score of 100 or more wins the game.
    • The winning player will be highlighted.

  5. Additional Rules:
    • The game alternates between the two players after each roll of 1 or hold action.
    • The dice image will be hidden at the start of the game and after each roll.
